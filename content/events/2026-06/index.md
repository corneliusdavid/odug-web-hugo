Janet Jackson asked this question in 1986. We're asking it of AI in 2026.

But unlike Janet's version, AI is getting better and is doing more every day. Not only is it speeding up debugging and development, it's also enabling the creation of tools that were previously relegated to wish lists.

<!--more-->

---

This month we'll be sharing what AI is allowing us to accomplish beyond the normal day-to-day work, projects that had been sitting on the back burner for a while simply because there weren't enough hours in the day or ideas of software that would save time but never came to life because it would take more time to develop than it would save.

AI is changing that equation!

Are you using AI to get more done? Whether it's debugging deep code more quickly, writing scripts you'd never have had time to tackle, or something else entirely, we want to hear about it. Come ready to share what AI has done for you lately. Even a 5-minute "here's what surprised me" is welcome. There will be at least three applications on display tonight, two in Delphi, one as an ASPX web page on a remote Windows server.

Join us for what should be a lively and practical discussion about how AI is reshaping the way we work and what we can accomplish.
